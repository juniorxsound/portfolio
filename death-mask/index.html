Death Mask

Augmented Reality / Machine Learning / Experiment


About

‘Death-Mask’ predicts how long people have to live and overlays that in the form of a “clock” above they’re heads in augmented reality. The project uses a machine learning model titled AgeNet for the prediction process. Once predicted it uses the average life expectancy in that location to try and estimate how long one has left. The aesthetic inspiration derives from the concept of death masks. These are sculptures meant to symbolize the death of a person by casting his face (i.e creating a mask).
