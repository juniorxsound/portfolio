### About

Stream curated holographic video directly from Vimeo into The Looking Glass.

Vimeo Creator Labs partnered with Brooklyn-based Looking Glass Factory to combine volumetric video with a medium that can display the content without a headset directly streaming from Vimeo.

We built an app that streams quilts (5x9 view videos) directly from Vimeo and reconstrucs them to a lenticular view on the disply, allowing more performent and seamless viewing of hours of content.

We also built a quilt recording tool using the Vimeo Unity SDK that allows creators to easily create, record and share this type of video to the app.
