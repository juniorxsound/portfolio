### About

To visualize the athletes’ movements, we used video and data captured during training sessions to create 3-D models.

**Motion Capture Data**: We used motion capture data acquired during training sessions to create 3-D models of the athletes’ movements.

**Video and Data**: We used video and data captured during training sessions to create 3-D models of the athletes’ movements.

**3-D Models**: We used 3-D models of the athletes’ movements to create an interactive piece that allows viewers to explore the athletes’ movements in 3-D.

**Augmented Reality Effect**: We used an augmented reality effect to allow viewers to explore the athletes’ movements in 3-D.
